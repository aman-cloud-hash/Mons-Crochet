Our Philosophy

The Art of Handcrafting

We believe that in a world of automated machines, there is a distinct beauty and warmth in things made patiently by human hands.

Mons Crochet began as a small passion project in our cozy living room. Crocheting was more than just a hobby—it was a form of meditation, where each loop, stitch, and pull of yarn transformed a simple strand into a delicate work of art.

Today, Mons Crochet is a premium destination for handmade crochet flowers, keychains, and custom hampers. We serve customers all over India who share our appreciation for luxury handicraft. Yet, our core values remain the same as the day we started: zero compromises on yarn quality, plenty of space for personalized designs, and pouring absolute love into every stitch.

Our Promise

Handmade Quality Standards

Premium Combed Yarn

We source premium milk cotton and combed cotton yarn, famous for its luxurious softness, tight stitching, and non-pilling texture.

No Layout Shifts or Sagging

Every piece is stuffed using high-density hypoallergenic fiberfill and supported internally with rust-free floral wire where needed.

Zero-Waste Packaging

We use eco-friendly gift wrapping box packs, tissue sheets, paper ribbons, and zero plastics to ensure a sustainable gifting experience.
