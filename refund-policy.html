Refund & Refund Policy

Home / Refund Policy
Last Updated: June 2026

1. Returns & Exchanges

Because all our items are handmade to order, we do not accept returns or exchanges for change of mind. However, your satisfaction is our highest priority! If your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact us on WhatsApp (+91 98765 43210) or Email (hello@monscrochet.com) within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the package and item. We will gladly send a replacement piece immediately.

2. Custom Order Cancellations

You can cancel your order within 12 hours of placement for a full refund. Since customization involves tailored yarn choices and immediate pattern drafting, cancellation requested after 12 hours will incur a 30% materials charge, and the remaining 70% will be refunded to your original payment method.

3. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days and will automatically be applied to your original credit card or net banking method.
