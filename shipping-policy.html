Shipping Policy

Home / Shipping Policy
Last Updated: June 2026

Thank you for supporting our small handmade brand. Here are the guidelines and expectations regarding our shipping and delivery processes.

1. Processing & Crafting Times

Unlike mass-produced items, every item from Mons Crochet is handmade to order. Standard items take 5-7 business days to craft, while custom orders may take 7-14 business days depending on our active queue. Once completed, your order is immediately packaged and dispatched.

2. Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates

We ship all over India using premium courier services (BlueDart, Delhivery, etc.). Estimates are as follows:

  • Metro Cities: 2-4 business days after dispatch.
  • Rest of India: 4-7 business days after dispatch.
  • Free Shipping: Available on all orders above ₹999. A flat rate of ₹80 applies to orders below ₹999.

3. Shipment Confirmation & Tracking

You will receive a shipment confirmation message on WhatsApp or Email containing your tracking number(s) as soon as the package is handed over to the courier partner.
