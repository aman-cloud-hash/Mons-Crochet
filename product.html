Product Care & Info

Care Instructions: Hand wash gently in cold water using a mild wool/cotton detergent. Lay flat to dry on a clean towel. Avoid twisting, wringing, or tumble drying to prevent stretching and loss of shape. Dust lightly with a soft brush for daily cleaning.

Eco-Friendly Crafting: All our crochet items are made in a smoke-free, pet-free environment. We focus on low-waste packing using biodegradable wrapping papers, cardboard boxes, and natural ribbons.

Shipping Details: Handcrafted to order. Please allow 5-7 business days for crafting before shipping. Free shipping is provided all across India.
